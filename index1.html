ABOUT

The IEEE Computer Society traces its origins back to 1946. After nearly 75 years, the organization now stands as the beacon of information and the premier source of inspiration and collaboration of computer science and engineering due to the 373,000 individuals from all around the world who have put their heart and soul into transforming it into the computer society that empowers people who advance technology by delivering tools for individuals at all stages of their professional careers. The IEEE Madras Section is one of the most active sections in India. St. Joseph's college of engineering has housed the student branch of IEEE computer society since 2002. The student branch is now a growing family of members who actively dedicate their time towards society and participate in multiple national as well as international events. VISION

To reimagine and reinvent technology in ways that help to build a fulfilling world, especially including the aspects of education, the community and the environment.

Mission

Our mission is to nurture and promote technological upheaval, meaningful innovation and excellence far and wide. To help inspire and accelerate the advent of augmented technological progress by producing and providing adequate tools as well as unprecedented value and opportunity for people, irrespective of their level of learning. To be the world's foremost provider of enriching technical information and tailored services to computing professionals throughout the world.
